Lasagna

Description:

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with savory meat sauce and lots of mozzarella too!

Ingredients:

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese (2 cups)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese (freshly grated)
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion (finely diced)
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles (plus extra in case of breakage)
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Prep Work

    1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients

  2. Make the Meat Sauce

    1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes.
    2. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high. Use a spatula to break up the meat so that it's very fine and crumbled. Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through. Add garlic during the last minute. Drain excess grease.
    3. Add the chicken broth, marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently while you boil the pasta noodles.

  3. Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles

    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
    2. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water for the lasagna noodles. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles to al dente according to package instructions. Gently stir with a wooden spoon throughout cooking. Drain and rinse with cold water.
    3. Lay the cooled noodles flat on parchment paper while you begin assembling the lasagna.

  4. Assemble

    1. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Next, add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them if needed.
    2. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1 and 1/2 cups of meat sauce.
    3. Top with 2 and 1/2 cups of Mozzarella cheese.
    4. Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray and place it spray-side-down on top of the baking dish to prevent the cheese from sticking to it.

  5. Bake

    1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
    2. Remove and garnish with fresh parsley. Let it sit for 15 minutes prior to serving to allow the layers to set.
