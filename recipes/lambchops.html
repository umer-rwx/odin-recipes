Lamb Chops

Description:

These lamb chops are easy to cook in the oven and are ready to serve in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 4 lamb chops (~4 oz. each)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon crushed fresh rosemary leaves
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup mint jelly

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220° C).
  2. Cut away excess fat from lamb chops. Coat each chop lightly in olive oil. Season lamb chops with garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Place lamb chops in a cast iron skillet.
  3. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven until cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from over and cover tightly with foil; let rest for 8 to 10 minutes. Serve with mint jelly on the side.
